Background

Where I grew up

I was born in Boston, Massachusetts, in 2003 and I am the youngst of three brothers. I was very quiet when I was young and found the most joy when I was playing sports. I started playing tennis when I was nine years old. In High School, I played on the tennis team my freshman year alongside my older brother Micheal.

In 2018, I moved to Las Vegas, Nevada, to focus on my tennis at the No Quit Tennis Academy so I could prepare to play college tennis. This would ultimately be the best decision I ever made because it led to me going to Concordia University Irvine in 2022.
